About Me

I am a certified UI designer based out of Charlotte, North Carolina. I create easy to use, aesthetically pleasant interfaces and I deliver quality work. I am passionate about design and visual expression is an important part of my life. I have expertise in Visual Design,Interface Design, Branding, Prototyping and Wire-framing.

I have a background in Quality and Business Analysis but I always wanted to be in designing field. So I took a break from my work and finished my certification in UI designing from CareerFoundry. Currently I am working as an UI/UX designer for MeetMonk company. My best qualities are analytical thinking, problem solving, determination, ability to Work in Harmony with Co-workers. I have experience working in Agile environment.

Besides UI designing I also love sketching, drawing and singing. I live with my husband and a beautiful 7 years old daughter.
