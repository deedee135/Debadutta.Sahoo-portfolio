About Me

I am a certified UI/UX designer based out of Charlotte, North Carolina. I create easy to use, aesthetically pleasant interfaces, and I deliver quality work. I am passionate about design, and visual expression is an important part of my life. I have expertise in Visual Design, Interface Design, Branding, Prototyping, and Wire-framing.

I have a background in business and quality analysis with the experience of over three years where I developed my expertise in software testing, creating user-flows, writing user stories, and I have a foundational understanding of SDLC, Waterfall, and Agile delivery methodologies. I offer a lot of experience in communicating with different kinds of clients and understanding their requirements. But my passion for designing led me to become a UI/UX designer. My analysis background helps me to design interfaces that solve the user's problems as well as fulfill the needs and requirements of clients and stakeholders. With knowledge of front-end development and interface designing, I aim to design products that are easy to use and aesthetically appealing.

Besides UI designing I also love sketching, drawing and singing. I live with my husband and a beautiful 7 years old daughter.
